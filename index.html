About Me

An Advertising Post Producer who found her way into Voice Acting!

Accent - neutral American English | Female | Age Range 20 - 35 | Non Union

Originally from the Midwest, Camila’s voice is naturally suited for all household, family friendly content. She brings the trust, care and spirit of America’s heartland into any script. From Automotive and Retail to Pharma and Wellness Camila has lent her talents to brands over a wide range of industries, and is always looking for a challenge!

As a Post Producer who specializes in social content, Camila has a deep understanding of how to both listen and speak to an audience, giving her a unique vantage point on how to connect with listeners making them feel seen and heard.
